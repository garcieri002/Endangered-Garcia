Sea Shepherd SSCS

Sea Shephard SSCS is an organization that was founded in 1977 in Vancouver, Canada. Their missions was to protect and conserver all marine wildlife. Today there are thousands of Sea Shephards in across 40 different countriess. The people at Sea Shepherd are known to be incorruptable, passionate, persistant, professional, cooperative, and effective. Together they fearlessly defend life and diversity in the oceans.

Their message is "We expose, intercept, and oppose illegal operations that damage and destroy marine wildlife and habitats."

Conservation

Sea Shepherds has pioneered a unique and effective approach to ocean conservation. Supported by their dedicated crews, they provide ships, equipment, technical advice, and consultation to multiple paretners, including local communities and government agencies around the world

According to them, their range is vast. They have successfully stopped poachers and habitat destructions in Berlin, Ecuador, Gabon, Indonesia, Italy, Liberia, Mexico, and multiple other countries. Their efforts are unrelenting. They have faciliated hundreds of arrests and the seizure of dozens of poaching vessels. They have recieved multiple awards including the Amazon Peace Award in Ecuador and the Distinguished Service Medal in Liberia and many others.

How you can help them

With any organization there are many ways you can help them. You may be wondering "How do I help them?". They thrive from our support. Our support is what drives their enforcement and conservation efforts. Their campaigns can't happen without your help.

Sea Shepherd is a non profit organization. When it comes to supporting them they say that no gift is too small to help. They accept donations and the donations are 100% tax-deductable. You can donate via mail, fax or even by phone. You can also donate online, which is safer with everything going on right now, with credit cards, bitcoin, or paypal. You can even enroll in a monthly donation plan. They also accept vehicle, estate, and stock donations. You can also make a fundraising page to help and support them.
